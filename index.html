About Me

I graduated from Temple University in 2017 with a Bachelor of Arts in Political Science. After realizing the disconnect between government, politics, and technology I enrolled at the University of Pennsylvania where I earned a certificate in Software Engineering.

I am adept in front and back end languages, responsive frameworks, databases, and best code practices. I am looking to utilize my technological skills in the intersection between government, politics, non-profit and technology.

As a first generation college student, immigrant, and female; hard work and grit are prominent qualities of my character. I am passionate about advocating for and helping underserved individuals and communities. I am a history and politics enthusiast who loves travel, animals, and family time.
