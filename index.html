MISSION STATEMENT

GirlCode's mission is to encourage young girls to pursue interests in Software Engineering, with an overarching goal of bridging the gap between males and females in STEM.

RESEARCH RESULTS

According to the NGC Project, female students' achievements in mathematics and science is on par with their male peers and female students participate in high level mathematica and science courses at similar rates ad their male peers, with the exception of cmputer science and engineering.

WHO WE ARE

GirCode is a community of like minded young females who are interested in Software Engineering. GirlCode serves as a platform where girls can learn about, explore, and get support in all things related to Software Engineering.
